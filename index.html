Who We Are

In a Few Words

360 Vision Design Corp. is a full-service global design, production, and logistics company offering comprehensive, turn-key solutions to businesses of all sizes around the world.

Backed by over 35 years of industry expertise, our dedicated team specializes in guiding products through every stage of their lifecycle — from vision, design, and development to sourcing, manufacturing, logistics, and branding.

We are committed to delivering the highest quality standards while maintaining sustainable value pricing, ensuring exceptional outcomes for our clients at every step.

What We Do

Every Stage. Every Detail.

We handle everything so you don't have to. From the first sketch to the final sale.

Market Study

Competitive brand analysis, consumer pricing intelligence, and audience segmentation to position your product effectively.

Brand DNA

We preserve and amplify your brand's identity — from color heritage and aesthetic direction to look-and-feel consistency across all touchpoints.

Trims & Branding

Design and planning of hangtags, main labels, logo prints, accessories, and packaging — all aligned with your brand identity.

Fabric Development

Extensive fabric library and vendor network. Recycled materials, special weights, and mixed-media — sourced to match your exact specifications.

Special Trims

Waterproof zippers, custom pullers, patches, and embroideries — distinctive details that set your product apart in a competitive market.

Tech Packs

Meticulous documentation — fabric details, color codes, dimensions, construction methods, stitching, and trim placements. The definitive blueprint for production.

Sampling

Full sample sets developed in actual colors, fabrics, and trims — with logos and print designs — for a 100% accurate bulk representation.

Production & QC

Rigorous vendor oversight at every production stage. Proactive inspections to optimize efficiency and maintain the highest quality standards throughout manufacturing.

Logistics

Labeling, special packing, and shipping — including direct delivery to specific countries. We cut logistics timing and costs with our factory network.

Photoshoot

Coordinated product photography that captures details, textures, and design features — perfectly aligned with your brand identity.

E-Commerce

Platform development and UX design that enhances the online shopping experience — built to drive engagement and conversions.

Retail & Shop-in-Shop

Fixture design, store layouts, and global fixture sourcing — leveraging our worldwide network for a distinctive, customized retail environment.

Visual Merchandising

Tailored solutions that enhance brand visibility and customer engagement — creating compelling in-store experiences that elevate retail presence.

Latin America Market Strategy

35 years of relationships with distributors and major retailers across Latin America. We guide clients through market entry, licensing, and DTC strategies in every country.
